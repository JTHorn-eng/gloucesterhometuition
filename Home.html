About me:

My name is William Taylor. I have over a year of experience in private tuition. In that time I have taught students from age 11-59 as well as those with learning difficulties. I know how to adapt to the needs of each student depending on both their ability and level of interest in the subject. Having recently completed both my A Levels and GCSE's, I have experience with the new reformed exams including the new content and style of question. This has allowed me to help students exceed their predicted grades in GCSE mathematics.

Teaching:

I teach all topics in the subjects listed below. I plan each lesson and, after initial assessment, will decide which topics to prioritise. Feedback is given to parents, if possible, at the end of every lesson so that you can keep track of your child's development.

Subjects:

GCSEs:

  • Maths
  • Physics
  • Chemistry

A Levels:

  • Maths
